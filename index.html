  • Language is the highest expression of human creativity.

    Language is not like history, or geography, or mathematics. Language is communication - a core tool in our human survival kit. It cannot be reduced into simple formulae - it is beautiful in its complexity. Learning should celebrate that.

  • Technology is a tool, not an end in itself.

    Technology is only of use if it helps us to do the things that we want to, better.
    Don't believe the futurologists: no technology will ever replace a good teacher - nor should it.
    Face-to-face teaching is the high point in education, the icing on the cake. Technology's role is to help when the teacher is not there, to enable a student to learn whenever they want. Technology creates fear, a divide between those who want change and those who don't. Change is not of value in and of itself, and neither is technology - what's important is what it helps us to achieve.
